Pembroke Welsh Corgis

Home About Why People Love Corgis

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is one of the two corgi breeds (the other is Cardigan Welsh Corgi) and is is best known as the Queen of England's breed of choice. These dogs have been bred for herding and with their short stature they are able to duck between cows and sheep to avoid being kicked or trampled. The color most associated with Pembroke Welsh Corgi fur is red. This is the most dominant corgi color and is often mixed with a white belly/paw/leg markings.
